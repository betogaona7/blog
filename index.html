Hi there!

I'm Alberto, a systems computer engineer and AI engineer who wants to improve in something the world. For that the only way I know is applying my knowledge, being a hand-on person.

The concept of this blog is exactly that: take action executing ideas, develop project after project and in each one publish posts from the development of the idea, the research, experiments, problems that arise, the ways in which they are resolved and the decisions that I make until reach a final product, be it a prototype, a paper or simply learning how not to do things (lose to win).

You can see this blog as a reference of how I develop and create things from scratch using deep learning or/and traditional AI, because that's what I love to do!
